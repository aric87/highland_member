More than 38 years of great music!

Founded in 1978, Highland Light is the oldest pipe band on Cape Cod. The band is available for hire as a Full Band Mini Band or can refer Individual Pipers and Drummers.

HLSPB welcomes new members, either experienced pipers and drummers or those looking to learn. Both bagpipe lessons and drum lessons are offered to prospective members over the age of 8.
